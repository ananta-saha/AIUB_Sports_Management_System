# AIUB_Sports_Management_System
AIUB Integrated Sports Information and Management System A university-level UI/UX design project developed at American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB). This system is a centralized platform where students can explore sports clubs, GYM facilities, events, trophies, and other sports-related information — all in one place.

About The Project The Integrated Sports Information and Management System is designed to serve as the central hub for all sports-related activities within AIUB. Students can browse available sports clubs and join them, view GYM facilities and membership details, stay updated on upcoming sports events, view trophies and achievements, and read official notices and announcements.

Features

Home Page — Landing page with sports highlights and navigation GYM — View gym facilities, schedules, and join membership Sports Events — Browse upcoming and past sports events Trophies — View university sports achievements Notices — Official announcements from the sports department Team Management — View and manage sports team rosters Contacts — Get in touch with sports department officials Sign Up / Login — Secure account creation and authentication About Us — Information about the sports department Popup Alerts — Success and error feedback messages

Pages Designed Home, Sign Up, Login, Events 1, Events 2, GYM, Trophies, Student Dashboard, Notices, Notices 2, About Us, Contacts, Team Management, Team Management 2, Popup, Popup 2

Tool Used Designed entirely in Figma using components, auto layout, and interactive prototyping.
